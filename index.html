About Us

Welcome to Hotel Jungle View, a tranquil retreat where nature and relaxation harmoniously blend. Nestled amidst lush greenery, we are a boutique hotel committed to providing our guests with a serene and unforgettable experience. Immerse yourself in the soothing sounds of the jungle, as you embark on a journey of rejuvenation and tranquility.

Jungle View strives to source local, sustainable and organic when possible. We work hard to source premium ingredients and we cook everything from scratch with love. We also do our best to pay our employees living wages (tips are shared with all employees, including kitchen staff) and to reduce our environmental footprint wherever we can. Overall, these factors translate to higher menu prices, but we hope that you find value and feel a sense of comfort in knowing that we aim to get better everyday at doing what is important to us.

However, Hotel Jungle View is not just about the aesthetics. We take great pride in curating exceptional experiences for our guests. Delight your taste buds with delectable culinary creations at our renowned restaurant, where skilled chefs blend local flavors with international influences. Explore the nearby hiking trails and witness the rich biodiversity of the surrounding jungle.
